Welcome to Napoli's

Classic Italian dishes, made with care, served with a smile.

At Napoli's Italian Restaurant, you’ll find generous portions, comforting recipes, and a team that treats you like family. From creamy pastas and rich tomato sauces to fresh-baked bread and indulgent desserts, every plate is prepared to be savored.

Whether you’re celebrating a special occasion or just craving a cozy dinner out, our dining room offers a warm, welcoming space to relax, connect, and enjoy a true taste of Italy right here in Tahlequah.
